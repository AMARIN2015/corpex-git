Accounting Software Selection & Implementation

With the number of accounting software packages available for small businesses and individuals growing every day, selecting one that is right for you has become increasingly difficult. As accounting professionals, we have knowledge of most programs. As a certified Sage, Quickbooks and various other program training office, Valente CPAs can help you avoid costly mistakes and implementation delays. Our experienced staff can help you choose and implement the system that best fits your needs as well as provide ongoing technical and accounting support to you and your accounting staff on demand. Solving accounting and software issue during the year makes for a smooth yearend process. Thinking of new software? Call us to help.
